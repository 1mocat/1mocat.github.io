Hello I'm

Chengquan Guo (郭城权)

I am an undergraduate student at Zhejiang University. I am also a research intern at the University of Chicago under the guidance of Prof. Bo Li. My research interests lie in AI safety, AI security, and Trustworthy Machine Learning.
